The World's First Vertical Theme Park

Rising 1,500 feet above Chicago's skyline, Despot Park will transform the former Trump Tower site into an unprecedented entertainment destination.

Experience the grandeur and gloom of history's great dictatorships through darkly humorous, tongue-in-cheek attractions that educate while they entertain. Each floor represents a different era of authoritarianism, realized with meticulous attention to historical detail and satirical storytelling.
